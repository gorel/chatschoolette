About ChatSchoolette

    Meeting new people isn't always to easy, especially when you're on a college campus of thousands of people! Chatschoolette is here to make this just a little bit easier for you! Chatschoolette will randomly assign you to a text or video chat partner exclusively from your College Campus. If the conversation goes well, you are also able to friend the other user and then message them later. Registration with an academic email is required so you know you won't be paired with those weirdos(ya know, like "The other guys" ;) ). Now get out there and go meet some new friends! Happy chatting!
